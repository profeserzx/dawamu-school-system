# 🏫 Aclass - School Management System

> "The all-in-one school management system built for Kenyan schools, by a Kenyan developer who understands the challenges firsthand."

## 📖 About

Aclass is a complete school management system born from 3 years of real-world experience at Dawamu School. It solves the everyday struggles of teachers, parents, and administrators with a single, powerful platform.

**[Read our full company story →](COMPANY_STORY.md)**

## ✨ Features

- **Admin Dashboard** - Complete school oversight
- **Teacher Portal** - Attendance, grades, assignments, student profiles
- **Parent Portal** - Real-time access to grades, attendance, fees
- **Fee Management** - Invoicing, M-Pesa integration, payment tracking
- **Analytics** - Performance and attendance trends
- **Timetable Management** - Class scheduling
- **SMS & Email Notifications** - Automated alerts
- **Graduation Management** - Track student progression
