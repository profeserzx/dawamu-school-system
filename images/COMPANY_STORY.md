# 🏫 Aclass - Company Story

## The Problem: Why Running a School in Kenya is Hard

For the past 3 years, I've watched my own school struggle with chaos disguised as "administration."

### The Daily Reality:
- **Teachers** waste hours manually recording attendance, calculating grades, and printing reports—time that should be spent *teaching*.
- **Parents** are left in the dark. They only find out about their child's performance during termly meetings, long after problems have escalated.
- **Administrators** juggle spreadsheets, paper files, and multiple apps that don't talk to each other. Fee collection is a nightmare—tracking who paid, who owes, and sending reminders manually.
- **Students** fall through the cracks because no one has a real-time view of their progress, attendance patterns, or behavioral trends.

I've seen teachers stay up until midnight marking exams. I've seen parents frustrated because they can't access their child's report card. I've seen admin staff overwhelmed by piles of paperwork when they should be focusing on improving the school.

**This isn't just inefficiency. It's a barrier to quality education.**

---

## The Solution: How Aclass Fixes It

After **3 years of building, testing, and refining** with real schools, Aclass is the answer I wish my school had all along.

### What Aclass Does:
| Problem | Aclass Solution |
| :--- | :--- |
| Teachers overwhelmed by admin work | **One dashboard** for attendance, grades, assignments, and student profiles—all in one place |
| Parents feeling disconnected | **Parent Portal** with real-time access to grades, attendance, fee status, and school announcements |
| Schools losing money on uncollected fees | **Automated invoicing, payment tracking, and M-Pesa integration**—parents pay instantly, schools get reports automatically |
| Data scattered across spreadsheets | **Centralized student information system**—every record, from enrollment to graduation, in one secure database |
| No insights for decision-making | **Analytics dashboard** that shows trends in performance, attendance, and finances—so schools can make data-driven decisions |

### Built Different:
I didn't build this in a vacuum. I built it **in my school, with my teachers, for my community**. Every feature was born from a real complaint, a real need, or a real "there has to be a better way" moment.

## The Vision: Where We're Going in 3 Years

Aclass started as a project to help my school. But I quickly realized: **every school in Kenya faces the same struggles.**

### Year 1 (Now - 2027):
- Launch Aclass as a **multi-tenant SaaS**—any school can sign up, customize their settings, and start using it within hours
- Onboard **50 schools** across Kenya
- Build a **community of educators** who share best practices and feature requests
- Become the **go-to system for中小 schools** that can't afford expensive enterprise solutions

### Year 2 (2028):
- Expand features: **Timetable optimization**, **exam analytics**, **teacher performance tracking**
- Partner with **Safaricom, banks, and government agencies** to streamline payments and reporting
- Reach **200 schools** and **100,000+ students**
- Launch a **mobile app** for parents and teachers

### Year 3 (2029):
- Become the **leading EdTech platform in East Africa**
- Expand to **Tanzania, Uganda, and Rwanda**
- Build an **ecosystem of third-party integrations**—libraries, transport, catering, and more
- Create **Aclass Foundation**—offering free access to underserved schools

### The Big Dream:
I want a future where:
- **No teacher** spends their weekend calculating grades
- **No parent** is surprised by their child's performance
- **No school** loses money due to manual tracking errors
- **Every student** gets the support they need, when they need it

**Education is the foundation of our future. Aclass is building the foundation for education.**
